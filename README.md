# cooperate-compete-dilemma

This is the infamous prisoner's dilemma. It is also known as the perpetrator's dilemma.

The great Yogi Berra suggests: when you get to the fork, take it! 

And he is right: at the core, the fork we get to seems to be whether to cooperate or to compete.

[bolt.new](https://bolt.new) helped me get this started. Deployed with [netlify](https://netlify.com). I am grateful to folks in both companies and [the free(dom for) software](https://fsf.org) like gcc/gdb that enabled them all.
